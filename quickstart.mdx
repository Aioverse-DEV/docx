## Quick Start Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you navigate and make the most of the Aioverse documentation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Explore the Navigation">
    Use the sidebar navigation to browse different sections including Knowledge Base, Blog, Wiki, and more.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use Search">
    Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) to open the search dialog and quickly find what you need.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Categories">
    Check out the different tabs at the top to switch between major sections like Knowledge Base, Blog, and Wiki.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contribute">
    Found something that could be improved? Click "Edit this page" to contribute directly on GitHub.
  </Step>
</Steps>
